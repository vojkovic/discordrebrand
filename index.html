About The Change

At Discord, our main motive has always been to bring people together. However, over the years, we've realised that video calls and group chats just aren't the way to do it. We've seen case after case of youth with heightened mental health issues due to our services. We can no longer deny that social media is harming our youth. The Discord of the future will cease this harming of our children and we can finally complete our goal of bringing people together.








Our Products

Discord is proud to release our latests products, a wide suite of cords. Yes, that's right. We're putting the cord back into Discord. No matter if you are a home consumer or professional, we'll be sure to make high quality cords you'll love. The sale of premimum quality cords commenses today. We are currently manufacturing VGA, DVI, fibre optic ethernet, USB, 5mm Audio, PS/2 and our flagship HDMI cables. This HDMI cable will revolutionise the industry. With our patented conductor material, a new range of video resolutions and refresh rates have been unlocked. Out of the box, this cord has the capacity to run an 8K display at 160Hz or 4K at 360Hz. Furthermore, this will be the first cord to support 16K resolution. Dynamic HDR formats are supported ensuring your display is true to life.


Goals

As mentioned before, we want to stop harming youth who are using our platform. With the conclusion of the 2021 Glasgow climate summit, we've reevaluated our commitment to the climate. We've doubled down on our sustainability by shutting down our costly fossil fuel powered server infrastructure. Furthermore, all cords produced by Discord will be 100% recycled and will be fair trade certified. The rare metals inside cables are often obtained from exploiting developing nations and children. We commit to working closely with our material suppliers, ensuring this event will not take place at Discord. Our last goal is to product high quality cables that are loved by a range of people.

Merchandise
